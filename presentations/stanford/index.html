Portals are evil

On making a campus intranet with Open Atrium

Kevin Miller - Cal State Monterey Bay - @kevinmiyar

About us (CSUMB)

  • Small liberal arts college disguised as a CSU
  • 5700 students
  • 1000 staff/faculty/amazing people
  • 12 grumpy tenured faculty
  • 1 developer, 1 project manager, 1 operations person
  • 1 new UX hire (yay!)

History of intranet

  • Tried uPortal, failed
  • Threatened with PeopleSoft portal
  • Built skunk-works Drupal 5 site
  • Moved to Google Apps
  • Did a huge discovery push in 2011
  • Chose Open Atrium
  • Now we are changing again!

What we found

Registrar requirements ≠ student needs

Efficacy of communication inversely proportional to length of message

With apologies in advance....

The long tail of interest

We are not Facebook
